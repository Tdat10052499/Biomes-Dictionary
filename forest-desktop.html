Forest Biome

Thông tin sa bàn

Global Coverage ~33.3% of Earth's land area
Recycled Materials Green plastic bottles (canopies), Cardboard & toilet paper rolls (trunks)
Climate Zones Tropical, Temperate, Boreal (highly variable)
Primary Flora Deciduous trees, conifers, ferns, mosses
Primary Fauna Birds, insects, deer, bears, big cats
