Ocean Biome

Thông tin sa bàn

Area Covers > 70% of Earth's surface and contains 97% of its volume.
Zones Intertidal, Pelagic, Benthic, and Abyssal.
Recycled Materials Layered blue plastic bags, blue bottle caps, and clear plastic wrap.
Primary Flora Phytoplankton, kelp, algae, seagrass
Primary Fauna Fish, whales, sharks, coral reefs, crustaceans
